Or how to do a lemon pie.

What you need :

  • 1 lemon (or 2 for extra atste)
  • 3 eggs
  • Shortcrust pastry
  • 100g of butter
  • 100g of sugar (or less if you want, but note that sugar counterbalances lemon's acidity)

Cooking instructions :

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Spread a little bit of butter on the mold, spread the dough and lay it in the mold.
  3. Put the sugar and the eggs together and whip the mix, while progressively adding the melted butter, until you have obtain a foamy mixture.
  4. Add the lemon juice and zest.
  5. Pour the curd onto the dough.
  6. Put in the oven for 30min. The curd should turn a golden color.
  7. Enjoy your pie !
